A digital garden is less a blog and more a... garden. Instead of polished,
chronological posts, notes start as **seedlings** and get tended over time.
Some grow into **evergreen** references, others stay half-finished sketches.

That fits `randomcommits.dev` perfectly: not everything here is done, and that's
the point.

## The growth stages

- 🌱 **Seedling**: a rough thought, freshly planted.
- 🌿 **Budding**: taking shape, still being revised.
- 🌳 **Evergreen**: stable and reasonably complete.
